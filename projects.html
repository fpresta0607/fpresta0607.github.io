Projects

Stoma and Peristomal Skin Complication Imaging Tool

At Hollister Incorporated, I led the development of a computer vision and machine learning-based tool for standardizing the measurement and segmentation of stoma and peristomal skin complications. Using smartphone camera images, the tool was able to batch analyze important features and display histogram statistics, while also providing transparent modeling of algorithm performance. The tool was presented to internal decision makers at Hollister Inc. to showcase its potential for product customization and telehealth applications, leading to a better understanding of imaging technology within the company. An IP of my algorithm is in the process of being filed.

Development of a Customized Epileptic Monitoring System

I am currently working on a collaborative project with the Neurosurgery department at the Medical College of Wisconsin (MCW) to design and implement a customized EEG recording system for invasive monitoring of epileptic patients. The system is designed to meet the unique needs of the patients and the department including mechanical adaptability, clean signal processing, and reliable memory storage. Clinical research, Dr. Hernan Rey, is currently conducting invasive experimentation, in which microelectrodes are implanted into patients with epilepsy who undergo a series of stimulation testing over the course of a several days. I am tasked with building a custom GUI to monitor critical device information and optimizing an ETL pipeline to automate the offloading of unstructured data, allowing for 24/7 data acquisition without manual intervention. The research project is gaining signifcant traction making it crucial to design a more efficient and reliable data acquisition process.

Evaluation and Optimization of a Natural Language Processing Model

I was responsible for the evaluation of an existing natural language processing model (BERT) at Hollister Incorporated's Futurism initiative. I documented the accuracy, limitations, and requirements for deep learning techniques such as topic modeling and clue estimation. I presented my findings to the leaders of the initiative, discussing steps for improvement, implementation, and upkeep of the machine. Additionally, I specified the skill set and expertise needed for further development, helping the company to take a more informed approach to natural language processing and deep learning.
